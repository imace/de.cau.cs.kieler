\______________________________________

A TMF Xtext version of the Graphviz Dot grammar. A parser and a serializer for
Dot files are generated from the grammar definition. Inspiration for this
grammar was taken from the openArchitectureWare Dot grammar:

http://www.openarchitectureware.org/pub/documentation/4.3.1/html/contents/visualization.html

The generated files are checked in and ready to use.
